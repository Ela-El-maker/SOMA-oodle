# PROMETHEUS — Strategist Lobe Knowledge Library

**Domain:** Goals, strategy, decisions, plans, outcomes, butterfly effects, risk/reward

This folder is PROMETHEUS's training dataset. Fine-tuning on this makes SOMA's
strategist lobe genuinely predictive — it learns which kinds of plans have worked
in *this specific project* and which have failed, not generic business strategy.

## What belongs here
- Goal outcomes (what was attempted, what succeeded, what failed and why)
- Strategic decisions and their downstream consequences
- Plans that required multiple steps — the full arc from intent to outcome
- Resource/priority tradeoffs that were made
- "Butterfly effect" moments — small decisions with large downstream impact

## Entry format (auto-generated by KnowledgeCuratorArbiter)
```
---
lobe: prometheus
type: [goal_outcome | strategic_decision | plan_retrospective | tradeoff]
source: [signal type that triggered this]
timestamp: ISO-8601
outcome: [success | partial | failure]
---
[content]
```

## Training threshold
KnowledgeCuratorArbiter will emit `training.threshold.ready` when this folder
reaches **100 entries**. OllamaAutoTrainer will propose a PROMETHEUS LoRA fine-tune
to Barry for human-gated promotion.
